  MEANING OF THE LYRICS    

        Pharrell Williams sings about staying positive in the face of bad news and adversity. 
        This is evident at one point in the song, he croons, "Here come bad news, talking this 
        and that... Well, I should probably warn ya, I'll be just fine." Later in the chorus, 
        he instructs the listener to express that joy and "clap along" in various ways to make 
        it known. It's an infectious, catchy song that you can't help singing along to when it 
        comes on.

        And the track's focus on happiness was very much intended.
