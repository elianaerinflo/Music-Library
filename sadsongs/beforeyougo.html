  MEANING OF THE LYRICS    

        Or another way of looking at it is that he is personally taking the blame for this individual leaving. 
        Sadly, Lewis Capaldi has revealed that the subject matter of this song is in fact suicide. Therefore 
        the sentiments he is relaying are from the perspective of a loved one that a victim of suicide has 
        left behind. And the loss of this individual is proving to be especially painful, as the singer 
        apparently perceives that he had an opportunity to prevent this from occurring but failed to do so. 

        So the title is based on him questioning this individual in that regard, as in if there was 
        'something he could have said' in the past to make him/her feel better and thus deter this person 
        from taking his own life. But of course if the addressee is indeed passed away, then this question 
        would be theoretically in nature, as in one the singer is asking himself in hindsight.
