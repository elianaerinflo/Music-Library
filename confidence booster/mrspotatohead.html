  MEANING OF THE LYRICS    

        Melanie references the 90s toy “Mrs Potatohead” which has several stick-on features to represent women 
        who alter their body parts with plastic surgeries. In doing so, she questions if they feel happy knowing 
        that such actions cannot be reversed. The reason she believes these women change their natural look is to
        feel more confident and fit in the media's portrayal of beauty. However, the end result is a permanent 
        feeling of uncertainty about one's own looks and whether a significant other will still be with them when 
        their altered parts begin to deteriorate.

        Lyrically, the song deals with plastic surgery, its consequences, and the struggle for beauty in the 
        modern world, especially for women.

        She explained that the song was not about her criticizing women who get plastic surgery, but rather
        questioning them on why they do it when they are actually more beautiful naturally.
